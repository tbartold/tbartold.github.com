Stanisław Muszyński and Konstancja Sibilska

Stanisław was born as Stanisław Mucha in 1854, in Solca Wielka parish, in Gębice. He married (1882) Konstancja Sybilska. He changed the family name to Muszyński sometime after he married. I'm not certain why he did so, but it appears that his grandfather was born with the name Muszyński in 1806. It may be that the two versions of the name were used interchangeably.

It appears that all of their children were born with the name Muszyński(a) in Ozorków.

  • Franciszek Muszyński (1884-1960) m. (1912) Władysława Kępska in Ozorków
  • Anna Muszyńska (1886-1934)
  • Władysław Muszyński (1888-1941)
  • Bronisława Muszyńska (1890-1891)
  • Wawrzyniec a.k.a. Wacław Muszyński (1893-1943) died 21-07-1943 in Oświęcim
  • Stanisław Muszyński (1895-1964)
  • Janina Muszyńska (1898-1996) m. (1922) Franciszek Sibilski (1885-1940) in Detroit
  • Marianna Muszyńska (1900)
  • Stefan Muszyński (1903-1980)
  • Edward Muszyński (1908-1942) died somewhere in Russia
