Thom's Lodz Province LDS FHC locality search helper.

This is a list of the links that anyone can use to get directly to the locality details pages for all the localities (320 of them) that LDS has online as of May 13, 1999 for "Lodz" Province (as defined in the pre-1975 map of Poland).

I have set up this list in two different ways. You are looking a heirarchical listing of the parishes. (Based on diocese - I think). Anyway it is a breakdown based on where in the Lodz Provice is located (i.e. Lodz, Piotrokow, etc.). The other way is alphabetic by name.

You can also check out

  • Warszawa Province Alphabetic Index
  • Warszawa Heirarchical Index
  • Lodz Province Alphabetic Index
  • Lodz Heirarchical Index
  • Poland Provinces Index

I hope this helps someone.
